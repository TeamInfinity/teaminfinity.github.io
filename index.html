About Infinity Kernel

Infinity Kernel is a custom kernel for Xiaomi Redmi 4A and Xiaomi Redmi 5A.

It's main goal is to maintain a good battery life without sacrificing (if not improving) performance.

Here's our XDA thread for Redmi 4A.
Here's our XDA thread for Redmi 5A.

Murali? Fabian? What/Who are those?

Xiaomi Redmi 5A users need not to worry. They can just use the one that says Redmi 5A.

For Redmi 4A users, here are the details:

Fabian uses different drivers than Murali's drivers.

If you are using:

a Treble ROM
Any AEX version that has a version number equal or higher than 20180602
or the LineageOS 15.1 that is published on XDA,

use the Fabian version.

Any other ROM/If you aren't sure, try the Murali one first. If it doesn't boot, try the Fabian one.

Steps to flash:

  1. Clear Kernel Auditor's data. (We recommend not using it at all on the stable channel.)
  2. Wipe "Dalvik Cache" and "Cache".
  3. Flash kernel.
  4. Reboot.

Any bugs? Any changelogs?

You can find our changelogs and bug list in our XDA thread for Redmi 4A or our XDA thread for Redmi 5A.
